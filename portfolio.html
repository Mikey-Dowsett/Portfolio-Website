Welcome to my Portfolio

I am a game developer, who loves to create immersive and engaging games with Unity. I started this journey in 2020 after watching Game Development videos. I have been fascinated by the power and flexibility of programming ever since. With every project I start I aim to learn something new or further develop my skills, whether or not I complete the project. I have a strong programming background, and so I typically try to focus on some artistic skill that I want to improve. I feel that my game 'Bouncy Ball' is a strong example of my improvement in creating a visually appealing game. My focus currently is to create games that tell more of a story that is interesting and engaging. Game development is more than just a hobby for me, it is a way of expressing myself and sharing my vision with others.
