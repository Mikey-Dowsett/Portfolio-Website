Bouncy Ball

Tools Used

Unity, Photoshop

Date Published

August 2023


Bouncy Ball started out as an idea I wanted to make in one week. It turned out to be a lot more fun than I expected and took two weeks to make in the end. The game play is simple, fire the bouncy ball to collect all the collectables. During this project, I learned about Unity's rule tile system and got a much deeper understanding of the particle system.
