Math Sweeper

Tools Used

Unity, Aseprite

Date Published

August 2022


Math Sweeper started as an attempt to recreate the original minesweeper. I created a randomly generating system that creates a certain amount of bombs when the player clicks their first tile, ensuring they always get one free tile. Once I had the main system done and working exactly like the original minesweeper I decided to add my own spin to it. I decided to give the player a chance to keep playing when they clicked a bomb. All they had to do was solve a math problem. Then I created a difficulty system off of that where each difficulty added more bombs and made the math harder by using bigger numbers.
